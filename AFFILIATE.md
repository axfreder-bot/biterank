## 1. DoorDash Affiliate Program

**Affiliate Network:** Impact (Impact.com)
**Application:** [affiliates.doordash.com](https://affiliates.doordash.com) or via Impact

### Commission Structure

DoorDash operates **three separate affiliate tracks**:

#### 🛒 Consumer Acquisition (Referring New Eaters)
- **Commission:** ~$3 flat fee per new customer's first qualifying order
- **Cookie Duration:** 14 days
- **Model:** CPA (Cost Per Action)

#### 🚗 Driver Acquisition (Referring New Dashers)
- **Commission:** ~$50 per new Dasher who completes activation requirements
- **Cookie Duration:** 14 days
- **Model:** CPA (Cost Per Action)
- **Note:** Payouts fluctuate seasonally; DoorDash often boosts driver commissions during labor shortages.

#### 🏪 Merchant Referral (Referring Restaurants)
- **Commission:** Up to **$1,000 per new restaurant** that joins DoorDash
- **Cookie Duration:** 30 days
- **Network:** Sometimes managed internally or via PartnerStack/UpPromote, but Impact is the main hub

### Program Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Program Type** | CPA (Cost Per Action), single-tier |
| **Cookie Duration** | 14 days (consumer/driver) / 30 days (merchant) |
| **Payout Frequency** | Monthly (Net 30 or Net 60, network-dependent) |
| **Payout Methods** | Direct Deposit (ACH), PayPal, Wire Transfer |
| **Minimum Payout** | ~$50 (network-dependent, ~$10 via Impact) |
| **Accepted Traffic** | Social media (TikTok, YouTube), blogs, review sites, email |
| **Accepted Countries** | US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand |
| **Excluded Content** | Adult, violence, illegal, religious/political content |

### Application Requirements
- Free to apply
- Primarily managed through **Impact.com**
- Content must align with DoorDash brand (no explicit or controversial content)
- Blog/site review preferred (not strictly required, but social media with engaged audience also accepted)

### Pros
- **Highest market share** in U.S. food delivery (~60%+) → highest conversion rates
- Multiple commission tracks (consumers, drivers, merchants)
- Strong brand recognition reduces friction
- Merchant referrals can be extremely lucrative ($1,000 per restaurant)

### Cons
- Consumer commission (~$3) is lower than some alternatives
- 14-day cookie is shorter than Grubhub/Uber Eats (30 days)
- Seasonal variability in driver commissions

## 2. Grubhub Affiliate Program

**Affiliate Network:** Impact Radius or FlexOffers
**Application:** [grubhub.com/eat/affiliate](https://www.grubhub.com/eat/affiliate)

### Commission Structure

- **Commission:** ~**$5 flat fee** per new diner's first qualifying order
- **Cookie Duration:** 30 days
- **Model:** CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
- Some sources indicate a **revenue share** component may also be available for qualifying affiliates

### Program Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Program Type** | CPA (Cost Per Action), single-tier |
| **Cookie Duration** | 30 days |
| **Payout Frequency** | Monthly |
| **Payout Methods** | Direct Deposit, PayPal, Check, Wire Transfer (via network) |
| **Minimum Payout** | ~$50–$100 (network-dependent) |
| **Accepted Traffic** | Websites, blogs, social media (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram), email (non-spam) |
| **Accepted Countries** | United States only |
| **Excluded Content** | Adult content, religious/political content |

### Application Requirements
- Free to apply
- Managed through major affiliate networks (Impact Radius, FlexOffers)
- Ideal for food bloggers, lifestyle content creators, and local city guides
- US-focused audience required

### Pros
- Longer cookie window (30 days vs DoorDash's 14)
- Strong urban market presence
- $5 per new customer is competitive
- Easy application process via major networks

### Cons
- **U.S. only** — not suitable for international BiteRank audiences
- Lower brand market share vs DoorDash
- Less diverse commission tracks (only consumer referrals)

## 3. Uber Eats / Uber Affiliate Program

**Affiliate Network:** Rakuten Advertising, Impact, Awin
**Application:** [uber.com/us/en/affiliate-program](https://www.uber.com/us/en/affiliate-program)

### Commission Structure

Uber operates a **combined Uber Eats + Rideshare** affiliate program (same program covers both).

#### 🍔 Uber Eats (Food Delivery)
- **Commission:** ~**$5–$10 per new user** who completes their first qualifying order
- **Cookie Duration:** 30 days
- **Model:** CPA (flat bounty per first order)

#### 🚗 Uber Rideshare
- **Commission:** ~$5–$20 per new rider
- **Drivers:** Up to $50–$200 per new driver activation

### Program Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Program Type** | PPA/PPL (Pay Per Acquisition / Lead), single-tier |
| **Cookie Duration** | 30 days |
| **Payout Frequency** | Monthly (Net-30 or Net-60) |
| **Payout Methods** | PayPal, Direct Deposit/ACH |
| **Minimum Payout** | ~$50–$250 (varies by network and region) |
| **Accepted Traffic** | Blogs, apps, social media (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), SEO content |
| **Accepted Countries** | Global (payouts vary by region) |
| **Excluded Content** | Explicit content, religious/political content |

### Application Requirements
- Free to apply
- **Selective on brand safety and content quality** — Uber is more strict than DoorDash
- Requires professional content with genuine engaged audience
- Blog or website preferred; social-only may be reviewed case-by-case
- Global program but higher standards for acceptance

### Pros
- **30-day cookie** — longest of the three
- Global brand; works for international audiences
- Uber Eats commission ($5–$10) is competitive
- Dual-track opportunity (rides + delivery) for broader monetization

### Cons
- **More selective** — harder to get approved than DoorDash or Grubhub
- Higher minimum payout ($50–$250)
- No merchant referral track
- No DoorDash-style $1,000 merchant bounty

## Strategic Recommendations for BiteRank

### Priority 1: DoorDash (Apply via Impact)
1. **Why:** Dominant market share = highest conversion rate for a food-ranking site. The merchant referral program is a hidden gem — $1,000 per restaurant is exceptional if BiteRank can attract restaurant owners.
2. **Action:** Apply to DoorDash on Impact.com immediately. Use BiteRank's food review/restaurant ranking content as your application pitch.
3. **Strategy:** Embed DoorDash links prominently on restaurant ranking pages. Consider a "Order on DoorDash" CTA on each restaurant listing.

### Priority 2: Uber Eats (Apply via Impact or Awin)
1. **Why:** As a complement to DoorDash. The 30-day cookie and slightly higher base commission make it a strong secondary partner. Global reach is valuable if BiteRank expands internationally.
2. **Action:** Apply after DoorDash approval. Some networks allow cross-approval.
3. **Strategy:** Use Uber Eats links for users who may prefer it (e.g., international users, or as an A/B test against DoorDash links).

### Priority 3: Grubhub (Apply via Impact Radius)
1. **Why:** Straightforward $5 CPA with 30-day cookie. Good for a third track, especially if BiteRank has strong urban content (Grubhub is strongest in major U.S. cities).
2. **Action:** Apply as a third, lower-priority partner. Keep it simple.
3. **Caveat:** US-only limits its value if BiteRank's audience is international.

### Monetization Tactics
- **Restaurant ranking pages:** Add affiliate links/CTAs for each listed restaurant with DoorDash as primary.
- **Driver/Courier content:** If BiteRank covers gig economy jobs, the DoorDash Driver referral ($50/referral) is a strong secondary income stream.
- **Merchant section:** If BiteRank offers restaurant profiles, consider pitching DoorDash's merchant program directly to listed restaurants.

### What NOT to Do
- Don't sign up for all three simultaneously — focus on DoorDash first, prove conversion, then expand.
- Don't rely solely on food delivery affiliates if BiteRank has broader content — diversify with complementary programs (meal kits, grocery delivery like Instacart).
- Don't expect merchant referral ($1,000) payouts to be easy — these require verified restaurant signups and typically have longer validation periods.
