### Full Description

**Stop searching for restaurants. Start searching for dishes.**

When you're craving ramen, you don't want "a Japanese restaurant." You want the best tonkotsu ramen. BiteRank is the only app that ranks restaurants by dish — not by star ratings for ambiance, but by what you actually ordered and whether it was worth it.

**Two ways to discover:**

🔍 **Dish Search**
Search any dish — "tacos al pastor," "ramen," "fish and chips" — and see ranked results based on real community reviews. Find exactly what you want, then order directly from the restaurant.

💕 **Food Swiper**
Not sure what you want? Food Swiper shows you restaurants, two at a time. Swipe right on what you'd eat. Get matches. Build your want-to-try list. Earn XP.

**The game layer:**
Every review earns XP. Level up from Foodie to Critic. Earn badges for being first to review ("Pioneer"), for consistency streaks, for covering new cuisines. Food discovery that actually sticks.

**Why BiteRank:**
• Dish-first rankings — find the best [dish], not just any restaurant
• Real community reviews, specific to dishes
• Gamified discovery that makes eating out more fun
• Direct DoorDash ordering links
• No ads, no algorithmic manipulation

BiteRank: We rank food, not ambiance.
